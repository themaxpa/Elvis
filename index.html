About Elvis Paul

Started my DJ career in early 2012 by working alongside and assisting some of the most experienced DJs in the industry.
Performing at Kochi’s busiest and most vibrant nightclubs and discotheques taught me the art of keeping the crowd grooving to energetic club tunes.
It also helped me develop the skills to manage diverse music genres and cater to a wide range of audiences, including both Indian and international crowds.

Presently working as a freelance DJ since 2012.

DJ Elvis is one of Kerala’s foremost and premier DJs, recognized as a trailblazer in exceptional music and entertainment today. His versatility across various music genres consistently creates an atmosphere of sophistication, talent, and celebration, captivating music enthusiasts everywhere. What sets DJ Elvis apart is his passion, originality, and individuality

His expertise extends beyond the DJ deck, earning him a reputation for remarkable musical acumen that has solidified his status as a distinguished DJ over the years. With an impressive understanding of music and mixing, DJ Elvis has mastered the art of transforming moods and ensuring nonstop enjoyment at every party.
